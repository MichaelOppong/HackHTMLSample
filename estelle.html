Favorite Creative Strategy

If you’re feeling discouraged, try breaking a task or problem into smaller tasks. When you get some traction with the sub-part of the problem, it can propel you to the next thing. I love a challenge, and when I approach them this way, they feel more friendly. There’s no problem too overwhelming when you can see it as consisting of more bite-sized problems!
