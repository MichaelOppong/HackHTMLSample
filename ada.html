Ada

Favorite Creative Strategy

Use ideas from everywhere! Follow your curiosity. Just start with anything you’re curious to explore and the connections will start sparking. For example, If you love ants, study ants! Check out the encyclopedia. See what the ants can teach you about whatever problem you’re trying to solve. Right now, I’m researching the physics of bounce. So naturally I’ve been watching a lot of basketball!
